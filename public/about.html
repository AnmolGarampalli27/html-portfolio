About Me

Anmol Garampalli is currently serving as a key member in the Founder's Office at Pandora Finance, where his daily responsibilities encompass the pivotal tasks of steering the company's strategy and propelling its overarching vision. In this role, Anmol plays a crucial part in shaping the trajectory of the company, involving himself in a spectrum of activities that contribute to the overall success and growth of Pandora Finance. His multifaceted responsibilities extend beyond mere strategy execution and vision-driving, involving a range of tasks essential for the effective functioning and advancement of the organization.

Before assuming his current position, Anmol Garampalli held a significant role as a Software Engineer at Smart IMS. During this tenure, he played an integral part in a dynamic team focused on developing an accelerator product tailored for Property and Casualty (P&C) Insurance Carriers in the North American region. His involvement in this project allowed him to contribute significantly to the technological advancements within the insurance sector, showcasing his technical expertise and dedication to impactful software development. Anmol's time at Smart IMS marked a period of professional growth and collaborative effort within an innovative team.

Anmol holds a degree in Computer Science from Guru Nanak Dev Engineering, emphasizing a strong educational foundation in his field. This academic background, combined with his hands-on experience as a Software Engineer and current strategic role at Pandora Finance, reflects a well-rounded professional equipped with both technical prowess and strategic acumen. Anmol's journey from a software engineer to a key figure in the Founder's Office underscores his versatility and commitment to making substantial contributions to the technology and finance sectors.
